Venture Out

Venture Out, affiliated with the University of Missouri, specializes in group team building through outdoor experiential education programs. We utilize our high ropes course, climbing tower and on-the-ground challenges to bring teams together.

I have been apart of Venture Out since the spring of 2020. After closing due to the COVID-19 pandemic, we have reopened and built our program back up. It requires 6 months of rigorous training before becoming a full fledged facilitator. Safety is the #1 priority and we are trained to keep a sharp eye on multiple individuals. Additionally, I am first-aid and CPR certified as well as trained for emergency situations.

I became the Lead Facilitator in the spring of 2022. This meant that I withheld more responsibility and leadership within the program. I am responsible for scheduling courses, training new staff, and addressing employee concerns. I meet with the coordinator weekly to plan team meetings and come up with ideas to improve the program.

Marching Mizzou Color Guard

I joined Marching Mizzou Color Guard in the spring of 2020. In the spring of 2022, I became one of four captains of the team. As a section leader, I guide individuals through practice and encourage positive attitudes. I remain quick on my feet and I'm responsible for problem-solving as situations arise.

I am apart of the Inclusivity, Diversity and Equality Committee in Marching Mizzou. We brainstorm ideas on how to make the organization more inclusive and welcoming for everyone.
